⚔ Boss 2

Dead Stars

Nereid, Triton, and Phobos — the second encounter of the Forked Tower: Blood.

🚧 This section's guide is currently under construction and not yet available.

📖 Overview

Dead Stars is a three-boss encounter fought simultaneously. The arena is a large star pattern divided into six wedges with inner and outer rings. All three bosses — Phobos, Triton, and Nereid — must be kept at roughly equal HP, as they equalize between phases.

At the start, Decisive Battle assigns every player a Gravity debuff tethering them to one boss (Phobosic, Tritonic, or Nereidic Gravity). Each player can only deal damage to their assigned boss for the remainder of the encounter. The outer death wall activates shortly after.

⚠ Mismatched debuff: If you receive a Gravity debuff for a boss other than the one you were standing near, move to that boss immediately. Tanks with a mismatch: wait until after Slice 'n' Dice before repositioning.

🧪 Phase 1 — Slime Phase (Three-body Problem 1)

Nereid and Triton transform into slime balls as Phobos jumps to center. Gravity debuffs are cleared — all players attack Phobos.

ℹ Primordial Chaos: Assigns 1–3 stacks of Ice Ooze or Nova Ooze to all players. Being hit by the blue slime removes Nova Ooze (or adds Ice Ooze). Being hit by the red slime removes Ice Ooze (or adds Nova Ooze). You must end up with zero stacks of both.
⚠ Frozen Fallout: Each slime telegraphs 4 jump locations on intercardinals. Being hit by both slimes simultaneously is fatal. The final jump always leaves one safe half of the arena.
☠ Noxious Nova: Lethal to anyone with any remaining Ice Ooze or Nova Ooze stacks — this damage pierces invulnerability. If you die here, do not accept a raise until after Vengeful Fire/Blizzard III resolves.
ℹ Vengeful Fire III & Blizzard III: Nereid and Triton jump to inner star corners and shoot massive conal AoEs toward Phobos. Stand in the blue region between the two bosses. Each hit adds a Thrice-come Ruin stack.

❄ Phase 2 — Snowball Phase (Three-body Problem 2)

Phobos and Triton become snowballs (immune until mechanic resolves) as Nereid jumps to center. All players attack Nereid.

⚠ Snowball Flight: Each snowball telegraphs 3 rolling line stacks. Letter parties (A/B/C) soak Frozen Triton (north); number parties (D/1, E/2, F/3) soak Frozen Phobos (southwest). Parties A/1 soak the 1st line, B/2 the 2nd, C/3 the 3rd. Tank stands at the front (wild charge). Each soak removes one Icebound Buffoonery stack — all 3 must be removed per snowball.
☠ Physical Vulnerability Up: Each snowball hit inflicts this debuff — you cannot soak two in a row without an invulnerability cooldown or you die.
⚠ Chilling Collision: One player per snowball is tethered with a distance tether. Nereid casts a massive central knockback. Tethered players ride the knockback to the far edge opposite their snowball. The stack (Avalaunch) deals proximity damage — be as close to the wall as possible.
☠ Self-destruct / To the Winds DPS check: Both snowballs must be defeated before the casts complete. If either snowball has any remaining Icebound Buffoonery stacks, a wipe is guaranteed — set up a Chemist Cheese at this point if needed.

🔥 Phase 3 — Fireball Phase (Three-body Problem 3)

Phobos and Nereid become targetable fireballs as Triton jumps to center. All players attack Triton. Gravity debuffs are removed permanently.

⚠ Fireball towers: Footprint arrows indicate where towers will form on the outer ring. Letters take the north/east fireball, numbers take the south/west fireball.
☠ Elemental Impact ×3: Each fireball slams into its tower 3 times, dealing heavy physical tankbuster damage + a conal Fire Spread on a random nearby healer/DPS. Tank the 1st hit with mitigation, then use invulnerability for hits 2 and 3.
⚠ Geothermal Rupture ×3 + Flame Thrower: Three ground-targeted circles at each player's position in sequence, plus a line stack on one random player per side. Move clockwise around the tower to bait circles, stay loosely stacked for the line stack, then return.
☠ Self-destruct / To the Winds DPS check: Both fireballs must be defeated before casts complete to restore Phobos and Nereid.

💀 Enrage — Six-Handed Fistfight

All three bosses jump to the center and merge into a single Dead Stars entity. The inner ring becomes permanently unsafe (standing in it deals damage, knocks you out, and adds a Thrice-come Ruin stack).

ℹ If all three bosses are at 0.1% HP before the merge, they immediately un-merge after Six-Handed Fistfight and the encounter ends. This is the earliest possible kill point.
⚠ Collateral Damage: Alternating conal AoEs on every other wedge of the star pattern, then small untelegraphed circles on one-third of players at a time (Magic Vuln Up). Spread within one star wedge — it is large enough for 8 players.
☠ Fusion Burst: Hard enrage. Kill the merged boss before this completes.
