The Binding Lock

The warded section between Marble Dragon and Magitaur. Also referred to as “Lockwards.”

🚧 This section's guide is currently under construction and not yet available.

📖 Overview

The Binding Lock section begins with an add pull (Guardian Petroglyph, Tower Hippocerf, Tower Skatene, Tower Marolith; note the Petroglyph is immune to instant kill effects), followed by six side rooms each containing a unique Guardian. All enemies here can be slowed.

After clearing all six Guardians, twelve players stand on the colored circles in each room to lower the six Binding Lock cylinders so their glowing lines flush with the ground. Then the authentication process begins.

✦ The Binding Lock area also contains 18 trap spawn locations (14 normal, 4 unstable). Coordinate pre-pop vs. reveal strategies; everyone must know the plan before proceeding.

⚔ Guardian Side Rooms

⚠ SW Guardian Wraith: Scream applies Terror (unable to move). Lingering Resentment then targets a random player, preferring terrified ones. The terrified player must be Rescued before the AoE fires or they die.
☠ W Guardian Berserker: Has undispellable Vulnerability Down and takes no meaningful damage from normal attacks. Must be killed via instant-kill effects only: Iainuki (Phantom Samurai), Phantom Fire (Phantom Cannoneer), Phantom Doom (Phantom Oracle), or Finisher (Phantom Gladiator). Pull simultaneously with the Wraith; one tank + all instant-kill players.
ℹ NW Guardian Golem: Pull with a single tank. Toxic Minerals inflicts a proximity AoE gas that converts to a cleansable 20-second Doom after 20 seconds. Healers Esuna the tank. Phantom Oracles can use Recuperation to cleanse the whole party while Toxic Minerals is active, allowing everyone to join the fight. Obliterate is a massive point-blank AoE; interrupt it.
⚠ NE Guardian Knight: Starts with a dispellable Invincibility. Phantom Time Mage uses Occult Dispel immediately. After Earthquake (donut AoE following Buster Knuckles point-blank), Invincibility is restored; Dispel again. Line of Fire fires an untelegraphed line AoE through walls at a random player.
⚠ E Guardian Bats ×3: Pull simultaneously with the Knight. Bats have extremely high evasion; use magical ranged LBs (cannot miss) and/or Phantom Aim to guarantee hits. Phantom Samurai's Zeninage also cannot miss.
☠ SE Guardian Weapon: Starts with Armed to the Teeth; every attack fires Keen Wind, a lethal AoE. Phantom Thief must use Pilfer Weapon (not Steal!) to remove the buff. Once disarmed, it alternates Whirl of Rage (point-blank) and Smite of Rage (line AoE).

🔐 Activating the Binding Lock

Each of the six side rooms has a large red circle. Standing on it lowers the corresponding cylinder by 1 level per person (up to 3 per circle). 12 players total must stand on the 6 circles such that all glowing lines become flush with the ground.

✦ The combination (e.g. "123 312" for NW W SW NE E SE) is typically called out by the raid lead. Once all cylinders are correct, the system message "Occult cylinders active. Initiate the authentication process." appears.

🔓 Authentication

Interact with the Authorization Device to begin a 60-second countdown. Waves of Lockwards spawn, each casting Cunning Keywork, which must be interrupted or the lock deactivates.

ℹ Short procedure (skip): When the 60-second countdown completes during wave 4, interact with the Unlock Mechanism. Lockwards despawn and the lock opens red. Fewer rewards.
ℹ Long procedure (full unlock): Ignore the Unlock Mechanism. From wave 5 onwards the spawn rate accelerates. In wave 8, a Master Lockward appears; do not interrupt its Cunning Keywork cast. When it completes, "The occult cylinders have been reordered." appears. Re-input the new combination with 12 players, then use the Unlock Mechanism. The lock opens blue. Unlocks Top Secret Nook 3 (5 Sanguinite) and spawns the Sanguine Recluse outside the tower.
