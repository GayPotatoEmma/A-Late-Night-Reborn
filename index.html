Main Guides
✦ FATE
01
Ovni
The notorious monster FATE required to open Arsenal entry portals. Earn gold participation before it dies to receive the Aetherically Primed buff needed to enter.

Art
One half of the Twin Spears duo. Art and Owain are aetherically linked and cannot be killed independently. The raid must split across both and bring them down at the exact same moment.

 ⚔ Boss 1
03
Owain
The other half of the Twin Spears duo. Owain and Art are aetherically linked and cannot be killed independently. The raid must split across both and bring them down at the exact same moment.

 ⚔ Boss 2
04
Raiden
A mounted thunderlord who electrifies the arena walls early in the fight. Survive exaflares, chain tethers, and spectral swords before his Ultimate Zantetsuken wipes the raid.
→
 ↗ Traversal
05
Hallways
The Arsenal's interconnecting passages are riddled with hidden traps and patrolling enemies. Watch your step and mind the mobs before pushing through to the next boss.
→
 ⬡ Support
06
Support Rooms
Six side rooms that must all be cleared to strip Absolute Virtue of its True Virtue super-buff. Each room locks on entry and spawns a wave of enemies when the switch is pressed.
→
 ⬡ Support
07
Tristitia
The Expedition Support FATE that spawns after all six rooms are cleared. Completing it respawns Absolute Virtue without its super-buff and opens eight new entry portals in Hydatos.
→
 ⚔ Boss 3
08
Absolute Virtue
A legendary superboss whose Eidos buff shifts between Light and Dark, altering its core mechanics. Engaging it before clearing the support rooms makes it impossible to kill.
→
 ⚔ Boss 4
09
Proto-Ozma
The Arsenal's final boss. A shape-shifting construct cycling through four forms, with Ozmashade clones mirroring its mechanics across all three platforms at once.
