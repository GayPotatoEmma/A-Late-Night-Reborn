Owain

One half of the Twin Spears. Owain and Art can't be killed independently, requiring the raid to split and coordinate across both arenas simultaneously.

Overview

Element: Fire & Ice

Art and Owain are aetherically linked and cannot be killed independently. The raid splits into two groups, one engaging Art and the other engaging Owain. If the players on one side die, the surviving boss will cross to the other arena and wipe the remaining group.

Owain's rotation is consistent and loops after the first full cycle. The loop begins at Pitfall and repeats after the second Spiritcull in the loop.

Most runs nowadays often will not see past the first Spiritcull. While it is nice to know how the later mechanics play out, it's often not required to spend much time learning these.

Elemental Shift / Elemental Magicks

  • Elemental Shift: Owain swaps between Fire and Ice elements.
    • Take note of his current element, as it determines how Elemental Magicks resolves.
  • Elemental Magicks: Owain performs a point-blank AoE below himself. Any spears matching his current element will do the same.
    • Move to a spear of the opposite element to avoid the AoE.

Spiritcull

  • Owain assigns either circular AoE markers or triangle stack markers to random players.
  • Players marked with triangles must stack together, as this attack deals heavy damage split among all players hit.
    • Being the only player in a triangle stack will deal heavy damage to you and possibly even kill you.
  • Players marked with circular AoEs must spread away from others to avoid overlap.

Pitfall

  • Owain vanishes and creates a proximity AoE. Owain reappears at the AoE's location when it resolves.
    • Move away from the AoE to reduce damage taken.

Hands

  • Two hands spawn and tether to random players. The hands will slowly move toward their tethered player unless that player is looking at them.
  • Hands gain a Defense Up buff when they are far from their tethered player.
    • Let the hands come close before focusing damage on them.
  • If left alive too long, the hands will explode and deal heavy damage to the group.
    • Prioritise killing both hands as quickly as possible.
