⚔ Boss 4

Magitaur

Infamy of Blood, the final encounter of the Forked Tower: Blood.

🚧 This section's guide is currently under construction and not yet available.

📖 Overview

Magitaur is the final guardian of the Forked Tower: Blood. The arena is an extremely large circle with three large outward-pointing squares separated by 30° wedges. Nine colored circles (pink/yellow/blue repeating) ring the outside.

Party assignments: Parties A & 1/D → NW square. Parties B & 2/E → South square. Parties C & 3/F → NE square. Being outside your square when not explicitly required is dangerous, as it will cause additional debuffs on yourself and others.

ℹ BAP Waymarks: Most NA groups use a specific waymark set per square for Assassin's Dagger. Each square has a "left waymark," "right waymark," and "center waymark." Place them precisely; a third-party plugin makes this easier.

⚡ Core Abilities

⚠ Unsealed Aura: Heavy raidwide magical damage. Requires heavy mitigation (Occult Mage Masher, Occult Unicorn, Mighty March).
ℹ Unseal: Magitaur unseals either its yellow axe or blue lance, granting Unsealed buff. Axe = auto-attacks target nearest 2 players per square. Lance = auto-attacks target furthest 2 players per square. Three sets of autos follow each Unseal.
☠ Assassin's Dagger: Three rotating dagger lines offset 120° apart. Daggers shoot out, return after 2 seconds, then shoot again rotated ~50° clockwise (6 shoots total). Being hit adds a Thrice-come Ruin stack. "North/right" or "south/left" pattern determines starting safe position (BAP waymarks). After the 3rd return, move to the center waymark for the remaining daggers.
⚠ Critical Axeblow / Lanceblow: Two of these are used during each Assassin's Dagger sequence. Axeblow safe zones = far from boss + inside squares. Lanceblow safe zones = near boss + between squares. The first resolves between dagger shoot 4 and return 4; the second after the final return.
⚠ Forked Fury: Tankbusters targeting the nearest and furthest player in each square simultaneously.
☠ Aura Burst / Holy (Conduits): Lethal raidwide unless the conduit adds that spawn at the start of the cast bar are defeated first. See Conduits section.

⚙ Conduits

Seven of the nine outer circles rise: all 3 yellow → Axe Empowerment Conduits (ring in middle), all 3 blue → Lance Empowerment Conduits (sphere in middle), 1 random pink → Universal Empowerment Conduit (both). The unsealed weapon determines which colored conduits must die.

☠ Arcane Recoil: When an Axe/Lance Conduit is defeated, it targets the nearest 2 players with a catastrophic hit. Two Phantom Knight tanks stand inside the conduit; one uses Pledge on themselves + their invulnerability, or Phantom Guard. If only one Phantom Knight per square, pre-assign a "pledge buddy" who accepts the hit with a personal invulnerability.
☠ Universal Conduit / Arcane Reaction: A single Phantom Berserker kills the Universal Conduit alone, standing between it and the wall. The Berserker must activate Rage before killing it. Arcane Reaction fires a line AoE at the nearest player and inflicts 1-minute Hysteria. Rage grants immunity to Hysteria.

🔄 Phase Breakdown

1
Opening + First Conduits
Unsealed Aura → Unseal (axe or lance) → 3 auto-attack sets → Conduits (Aura Burst or Holy)
2
Sage's Staff
Three Sage's Staves appear → Critical Axeblow/Lanceblow → Mana Expulsion line stacks → three more Staves appear → Critical Axeblow/Lanceblow (opposite) → line stacks → Unsealed Aura
3
Rune Axe
Unseal (always axe) → 3 auto sets (nearest 2) → Rune Axe: Axebit debuffs resolve in three waves (10s Greater → 13s Lesser → 21s both) → Critical Lanceblow mid-sequence → Carving Rune → Forked Fury → Aura Burst Conduits (Axe)
4
Holy Lance
Assassin's Dagger → Unseal (always lance) → 3 auto sets (furthest 2) → Holy Lance: 12 Luminous Lances fire Lancelight in sequence + Lancepoint stacks → Critical Axeblow → Unsealed Aura → Forked Fury → Holy Conduits (Lance) → Assassin's Dagger → Unsealed Aura (enrage)

💡 Tips & Tricks

✦ For Rune Axe: the 10-second Greater Axebit player runs to the edge behind the nearest pink circle immediately on the cast. Remaining players with 13-second Lesser Axebit stand at their square's center and do not move until their AoE resolves.
✦ For Holy Lance: Lances fire in clockwise order (1→12). When the lances inside your square are firing, move to the back of the square. Return immediately after lance 4/8/12 passes. Your Lancepoint stack resolves outside when your square is "hot."
⚠ For Sage's Staff, the simpler NA strat ("A staff" or "Spiral stack") has everyone collapse to a single point, typically the A square's left waymark. Damage is distributed across the whole raid so each stack is survivable.
✦ Resurrection debuffs are not cleared after Magitaur dies. Any player with Resurrection Denied at kill cannot loot the personal spoils coffin or activate the Survey Point.
