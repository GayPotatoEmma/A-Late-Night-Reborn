Art

One half of the Twin Spears. Art and Owain can't be killed independently, requiring the raid to split and coordinate across both arenas simultaneously.

Overview

Element: Earth

Art and Owain are aetherically linked and cannot be killed independently. The raid splits into two groups, one engaging Art and the other engaging Owain. If the players on one side die, the surviving boss will cross to the other arena and wipe the remaining group.

Art's rotation is consistent and loops after the first full cycle. The loop begins at Legendary Geas and repeats through to Piercing Dark.

Most runs nowadays often will not see Legendary Geas and beyond. While it is nice to know how these play out, it's often not required to spend much time learning these.

Legendcarver / Legendspinner

  • Legendcarver: A point-blank AoE centred on Art that deals heavy damage.
    • Move away from Art before this resolves.
  • Legendspinner: A donut AoE centred on Art that deals heavy damage.
    • Move close to Art before this resolves.

Mythcall

  • Art summons a set of 4 spears at either the cardinals or intercardinals of the arena.
  • The spears will copy Art's current Legend ability.
    • Mythcarver: A point-blank AoE below each spear. Move away from all spears.
    • Mythspinner: A donut AoE below each spear. Move close to one of the spears.

Legendary Geas

  • Art spawns an AoE below a random player. Move out of it before it resolves.
  • The floor turns dark. Any movement during this phase applies a Heavy debuff and a Bleed.
    • Stop moving until the floor returns to normal.
  • Once the floor is safe again, a star-shaped line AoE spawns where the initial AoE was dropped. Move out of it.

Pitfall / Glory Unearthed

  • Pitfall: Art vanishes and creates a proximity AoE. Art reappears at the AoE's location when it resolves.
    • Move away from the AoE to reduce damage taken.
  • Glory Unearthed: A large AoE ball chases a single player and deals moderate damage in a wide area.
    • If you are being chased, move to the edge of the arena to keep it away from the group.
    • If you are not being chased, stay well away from the marked player.

Piercing Dark

  • A circular AoE is placed on 2 random players.
  • Move away from other players so the AoEs do not overlap.
    • If you are not targeted, move away from the marked players.
