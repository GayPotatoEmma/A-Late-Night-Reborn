Central Passages

The hallway section between Demon Tablet and Dead Stars. Also referred to as “Hallways.”

🚧 This section's guide is currently under construction and not yet available.

📖 Overview

After defeating Demon Tablet, the raid splits into two groups at the transporter — letter parties (A, B, C) take the left passage, number parties (D/1, E/2, F/3) take the right. Each side navigates a mirrored layout of hallways containing hidden explosive traps and dangerous enemy pulls, then rejoins at the Dead Stars arena.

Every avoidable enemy AoE in this section is instantly lethal. Your lives are limited — do not take unnecessary risks. Stay behind your Thieves and Rangers at all times in trap areas.

💣 Hidden Explosive Traps

☠ Normal Trap: Small lethal AoE on detonation. Phantom Thief uses Trap Detection to reveal, then Phantom Ranger uses Occult Falcon to disarm at range — or a tank runs it with invulnerability active.
☠ Unstable (Super) Trap: Massive wipe-level AoE. Do not disarm with Occult Falcon. Only a tank using a full invulnerability ability can safely disarm one. All raid members must be notified and moved far away first.
✦ Trap areas are marked by glyphs emanating from a thick red line on the floor. The glyph side contains the traps. Your Thief should always be ahead revealing the path.

👾 Notable Enemies

⚠ Tower Manticore: Casts Left Hammer or Right Hammer — a 180° cleave in the named direction, then immediately follows with the opposite direction. Dodge to the first unsafe side. Use Phantom Time Mage's Occult Slowga to extend the telegraph window.
⚠ Tower Scarab: Uses Rhino Guard, granting Evasion Up — Phantom Time Mage must Occult Dispel this immediately or attacks miss. Vulnerable to Slow, Blind, and Doom.
ℹ Silent Orbs: Patrolling green orbs with a point-blank AoE field. Walking into one inflicts a 60-second uncleansable Silence. Casters and healers must keep clear at all times.
✦ General mobs: All enemies respond to Stun, Sleep, Slow, and Doom (varies by type). Use Occult Slowga on every pull to give the group time to react to AoEs. Tank enmity first — mob auto-attacks hit hard.

🎁 Loot & Secret Rooms

✦ Thief's Trick Room: A Phantom Thief can reveal a hidden passage to a locked room on their side, yielding 2 Sanguinite per chest.
✦ North Secret Rooms: One of the two northern secret rooms per side contains a chest with 3 Sanguinite. Groups can choose to collect or skip.
ℹ Players can only access their own side's chests. A full-loot pass of both sides yields up to 10 Sanguinite total across the raid.
